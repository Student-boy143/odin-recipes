Mumbai's Special

Description

Pav Bhaji is a popular Indian street food consisting of a spicy vegetable mash (bhaji) served with buttered, toasted bread rolls (pav). The bhaji is made by cooking vegetables like potatoes, peas, carrots, and bell peppers, which are then mashed and cooked with various spices. It is typically served with chopped onions, a wedge of lemon, and a dollop of butter. The dish is flavorful, aromatic, and enjoyed as a snack or meal, especially in Mumbai and other parts of India.

  • Butter
