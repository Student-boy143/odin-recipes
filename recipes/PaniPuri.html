Description

Pani Puri, also known as Golgappa or Phuchkas, is a popular street food in India. It consists of crispy, round hollow puris filled with a combination of tangy, spicy water (pani), and a mix of potatoes, chickpeas, and various chutneys. The puris are delicate and crunchy, and the burst of flavorful liquid inside provides a unique, mouthwatering experience.
